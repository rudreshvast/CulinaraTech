# FoodTech Academy: Bridging the Industry Gap

## Project Overview
A modern Edutech platform designed for the Indian food processing industry. It connects college graduates with industry-led training, short courses, and apprenticeship programs to bridge the employability gap.

## Target Audience
- **Students/Graduates:** Seeking practical skills and industry exposure.
- **Industry Partners:** Companies looking to train and scout talent.

## Key Features
- **Industry-Led Courses:** Practical training modules designed by food industry veterans.
- **Apprenticeship Portal:** Direct connection to short-term industry placements.
- **Expert Directory:** Access to industry mentors and instructors.
- **Progress Tracking:** Modern dashboard for students to monitor their learning journey.

## Design Principles (Cyber-Culinary)
- **Futuristic & Modern:** Sleek UI using React, Tailwind, and Shadcn.
- **Youthful Energy:** Vibrant color accents and intuitive navigation.
- **Functional Clarity:** Drawing structural inspiration from the PE Association model but with a contemporary 2024 tech aesthetic.

## Proposed Screens
1. **Home / Landing Page:** High-impact hero section, featured courses, industry partners, and "Why FoodTech?" section.
2. **Course Catalog:** Searchable and filterable grid of industry-led training and certifications.
3. **Apprenticeship & Jobs Board:** Listings for short-term programs and entry-level roles in food processing.
4. **Course Detail Page:** Syllabus overview, instructor bios, and clear "Enroll Now" call-to-action.
5. **Student Dashboard:** Personalized view of active courses, certificates earned, and recommended programs.
6. **Instructor/Expert Portal:** For industry pros to manage their courses and interact with students.
